Skill Development for Livelihood

This module focuses on helping women gain hands-on skills that can directly lead to income-generating activities in their communities.

Topics covered include:

  • Tailoring and stitching basics for local markets
  • Creating and selling handicrafts
  • Agricultural techniques and home-based farming
  • Packaging and presentation of handmade products
  • Local marketing and digital sales techniques

By mastering these skills, women can begin supporting themselves and their families through sustainable and dignified livelihoods.
