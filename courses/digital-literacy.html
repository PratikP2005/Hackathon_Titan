Digital Literacy & Internet Skills

About the Course

Master the basics of using smartphones, browsing the internet, online payments, and accessing digital services safely. This course is designed to help rural women become confident and independent digital users.

What You Will Learn

  • Using smartphones and tablets
  • Understanding mobile data and Wi-Fi
  • Searching information online
  • Creating and using email
  • Safe online banking and UPI payments
  • Accessing government digital services
