Soft Skills & Communication

This module focuses on enhancing the interpersonal and communication skills essential for personal and professional growth.

Topics covered include:

  • Effective verbal and non-verbal communication
  • Listening skills and building empathy
  • Public speaking and confidence building
  • Workplace etiquette and teamwork
  • Time management and personal development

Mastering soft skills can help women feel more confident in expressing ideas, leading initiatives, and succeeding in interviews or workplace settings.
